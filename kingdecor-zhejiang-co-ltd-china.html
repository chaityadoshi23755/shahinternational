About Kingdecor

Located at Qujiang Economic Development Zone of Quzhou City in Zhejiang Province, Kingdecor (Zhejiang) Co., Ltd. was established by Schattdecor AG in Germany and Zhejiang Xianhe Special Paper Co., Ltd. as a joint venture on September 13, 2004. The company is mainly engaged in the production and sale of decor base paper.

Shah International introduced KINGDECOR with a range of products during late 2007 in Indian market. Within a short span of 8 years, Kingdecor has grown leaps and bounds and now have annual capacity of 120,000 MT of production capacity for base paper. They are the biggest company in Asia and world's third biggest producer of base paper.

Products

1. Solid Base Paper
Kingdecor offers wide-range of eco-friendly solid base paper shades of top-grade quality. Available from 60 gsm to 115 gsm in width varying from 123 cms to 186 cms.

2. Print Base Paper
Kingdecor offers print base paper used for décor printing which is available in different gsm from 50 gsm to 150 gsm.

3. Tissue Paper
Kingdecor introduces tissue paper for décor surface wear resistance available in 18 gsm. Features minimum tolerance in gsm, good whiteness and transparency.

Teamwork

In establishing the joint venture, Schattdecor AG and Zhejiang Xianhe Special Paper Co. had to overcome many geographical, linguistic and cultural barriers, resulting in the most advanced décor base paper production facility in the world.

Kingdecor takes a stringent approach in the sourcing of raw materials and is committed to the standardization of production process.
