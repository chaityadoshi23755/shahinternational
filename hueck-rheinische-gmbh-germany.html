The origin of HUECK Rheinische GmbH goes back to the merger of HUECK and Rheinische Press Pad GmbH. HUECK, founded by Eduard HUECK in 1814, has a long tradition in metal forming and surface finishing technology.

The newly founded company HUECK Rheinische GmbH is focused on the production of press pads and the design development of surfaces, research and development of new materials and surfaces.

Products

Press Plates — Textured, smooth, hard-chromed press plates. HUECK Rheinische GmbH is a leading company in the manufacture of laminated panels and decorative laminates.

Press Pads — For short cycle and multi day light presses. For very different circumstances a standardized press pad cannot be the optimal solution. HUECK Rheinische therefore offers customized solutions.

Patents

HUECK Rheinische GmbH is heavily involved in research and development of new materials and surfaces to always offer new and innovative ideas to the industry.

Services

HUECK Rheinische GmbH offers well-rounded services due to their decades of experience and technologies around surface development and press pad manufacturing.
