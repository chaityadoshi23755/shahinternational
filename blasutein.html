blasutein

When developing the décor, the designers worked with the original material of a Belgian Blue Stone - hence the name. The specialty of this stone: small, fine inclusions of snails, corals or other fossils. This stone radiates something very special: wild, spellbound vitality and a touch of history, in an overall homogeneous look. It consists, so to speak, of many small originals. The decor is ideal for interior design, but works well as a stone decor, especially on tabletops in kitchens or on bathroom furniture.
