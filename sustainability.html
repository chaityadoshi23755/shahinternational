Commitment to Our Planet

Pioneering eco-friendly solutions in the Indian laminate and decor industry.

Certified Responsible Sourcing

We believe that true innovation in interior architecture must not come at the cost of our forests. We ensure that all raw materials and decor papers supplied through our global partners are strictly FSC (Forest Stewardship Council) compliant.

Global Partner Alignment

Aligned with global progress initiatives, such as the Schattdecor Progress Report on Sustainability 2025, we work exclusively with principals who implement UDR compliant standards, utilize water-based organic inks, and prioritize circular economy principles.
