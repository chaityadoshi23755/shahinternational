Pyrus Panels, Germany

About

Pyrus Panels GmbH is the engineering and producing company of the high-performance material lisocore®. The company headquarters as well as the production site is situated in Bad Aibling, Germany.

During the extraordinary year 2020, Pyrus Panels GmbH (formerly known as Lightweight Solutions GmbH) has successfully completed extensive reorientation and restructuring.

Product Information — lisocore®

lisocore® is a highly efficient material. Through its unique structure this state-of-the-art, future-oriented material achieves outstanding performance characteristics.

lisocore® also convinces through its material efficiency and sustainability. During production, two thirds of the raw material is saved compared to conventional panels.

In 2020, lisocore® was awarded with the Green Product Award and was among the finalists of the "Deutscher Nachhaltigkeitspreis 2021"!
