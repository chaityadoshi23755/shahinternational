arno walnut

The designers’ approach is just as exotic as the name of this real material decor: a variety of walnut woods has been combined to create Arno Walnut. The result is a multifaceted decor with a walnut look. Very linear and featuring slight movements in the growth rings, this design presents a fine balance between tranquil and dynamic sections, which is further emphasized by a strong play of colors.
