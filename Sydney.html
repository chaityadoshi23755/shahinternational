Sydney

With the Sydney decor, too, love is in the detail. The stone decor consists of differently colored natural stone fragments embedded in cement structures in different sizes that form and emphasize a playful yet very subtle decor character. It's unassuming and timeless. Due to the reduced details and the simple design, it can unfold its full effect on large areas, but also exude great modernity on worktops. It is up to the viewer to decide which stone it is.
