The company was founded by the grand vision of one man, Mr. Kirti Shah in 1993, is today a highly reputed company in the industry. Mr. Kirti Shah has spent more than 35 years in the industry and no need to say he shares his experience working with even 3rd generations of our customers. Since then we have grown by leaps and bounds at Shah International. Today, we represent several world renowned companies in the Indian subcontinent as an indenting company. We build commercial relations between our principals and our Indian clients by supplying raw materials to the manufacturers of High Pressure Laminate (HPL) and Pre-laminated particle boards used in the readymade furniture industry.
