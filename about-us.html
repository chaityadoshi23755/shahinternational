A Legacy of Excellence

At Shah International, it has always been our endeavor to be a world-class service oriented company and provide an excellent service to our principals and customers. And in years to come, we shall further integrate businesses across countries and build ties that go beyond mere business relations.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading Indenting house providing high quality service to our valued customers and esteemed principals in the laminate and wood based panel industry.

Our Mission

To maximize customer satisfaction and our principals value through best management practices and continually striving for excellence in the services we provide.

Truly said "Together we grow"

We feel proud to share with you that we have completed more than 25 years in the industry. We want to share this success with our valued customers whose objective constantly helped us to nourish the seed of aspiration and inspiration within us.

The company was founded by the grand vision of one man, Mr. Kirti Shah in 1993, is today a highly reputed company in the industry. Mr. Kirti Shah has spent more than 35 years in the industry and no need to say he shares his experience working with even 3rd generations of our customers.

Today, we represent several world renowned companies in the Indian subcontinent as an indenting company. We provide one stop solution to the manufactures of HPL and MFC by representing the following world renowned companies for India and subcontinent.
