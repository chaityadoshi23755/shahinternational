Deurowood GmbH, Austria

We are introducing Deurowood Productions GmbH, located near lake Constance in Hard, Austria. Deurowood serves the global impregnation market since many years. Since 2017 Deurowood, together with Shah International, has established a strong presence in India.

Eco-Friendly

An important aspect is the protection of nature. The additives are constantly being developed from an ecological point. Deurowood Production GmbH contributes toward a healthy environment.

Products

Additives for paper impregnation

Deurowood offers a comprehensive range of additives for the impregnation industry including release agents, wetting agents, hardeners, and specialty chemicals.

Laboratory and Application

Very modern lab equipment for analyses in various areas enables Deurowood to provide precise testing and quality control for all their products.

Performance

Deurowood's products are designed to enhance the performance and quality of the impregnation process, delivering consistent results across different production environments.
