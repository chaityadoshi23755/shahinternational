Introduction

Mitsubishi Chemical Corporation (MCC) has a wide reach with 3 branch offices, 9 R&D centers, 15 plants, 4 regional headquarters in different parts of the world.

About

MCC, a core operating company of the Mitsubishi Chemical Group (MCG), Japan, seeks to provide solutions to issues facing society and the environment through innovation in chemistry.

MCC has been engaged since 1975 in the development, manufacture, and sale of acrylic (PMMA) films through a combination of their unique in-house polymerization and film casting technologies.

Product — ACRYPLENT

ACRYPLENT (acrylic film) features excellent transparency, weatherability, suitability for heat-molding and lamination processing. In addition, it serves as an exterior UV protection film for building materials.

Applications

Construction materials — Exterior protection for HPL panels, window profiles, and other building materials exposed to UV radiation and harsh weather conditions.
