Flagstaff Oak

This oak decor impresses with its old wood look. A special highlight: striking cracks that are not black, as usual, but silver. The result: a special pewter look that gives the decor a metallic character in parts. Overall, the design is lively and looks rustic due to this special feature. As a furniture decor, it can be beautifully combined and yet adds a sophisticated note.
