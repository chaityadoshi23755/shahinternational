Zebrano

This exotic woodgrain originally comes from west Africa and offers irregular, slightly wavy stripes resulting from alternate sections of dark and pale wood. Subtle poring and a slightly fibrous texture add a touch of their own. For a number of years, Zebrano has returned to being a popular trend decor for residential furniture with a high-quality elegant look or a young and fresh appeal.
